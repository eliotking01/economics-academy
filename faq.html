Marking Services

All questions are marked by Eliot King, an expert Economics Tutor with extensive experience teaching A-Level Economics and marking exam papers. For more information about my qualifications and experience, please visit our About page.

You will receive:

  • A mark for each answer submitted (and a grade if submitting an entire paper)
  • Detailed feedback on every question
  • Specific comments on Knowledge, Application, Analysis and Evaluation
  • Essay structure and technique feedback
  • Clear guidance on how to improve
  • A summary of key areas to focus on in your revision

After completing your purchase, you can submit your work via email. We accept:

  • Scanned copies (PDF or image files)
  • Word documents
  • Photos of handwritten work (clear and legible)
  • Google Docs links (make sure sharing permissions are set correctly)

You'll receive submission instructions immediately after payment.

Standard turnaround time is 48 hours from when we receive your work. During peak periods (especially near exam dates), this may extend to 72 hours. If you need expedited marking (24-hour turnaround), please contact us before purchasing as this may be available for an additional fee.

Most of our resources are designed to be useful across all exam boards, as the core economics content is similar. However, we clearly label resources that are specifically tailored to:

When browsing resources, you can filter by exam board to find the most relevant materials for your needs.

Due to the digital nature of our resources, we generally don't offer refunds once materials have been downloaded. However:
