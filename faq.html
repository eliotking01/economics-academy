About Economics Academy

Economics Academy is a specialist A-Level Economics site run by Eliot King. It offers free revision notes and past papers for Edexcel, AQA, and OCR students — alongside paid 1-on-1 online tutoring and a paper marking service with expert KAEA feedback.

Eliot King holds a First-Class BSc in Economics from the University of Bath and has 6 years of tutoring experience with over 100 students taught. He is rated 5/5 on Tutorful and specialises in exam technique, essay structure, and content knowledge across Edexcel, AQA, and OCR.

Marking Service

  • Single 25-mark question — £25
  • Three 25-mark questions — £60 (saving £15 vs the single rate)
  • Full exam paper — £60 (all question types marked, overall grade included)

Select a package on the Marking page and pay securely via Stripe. Submission instructions are sent immediately after payment.

All marking is done by Eliot King — the same tutor who runs every session on the site. First-Class BSc Economics from the University of Bath, 6 years of tutoring experience, 100+ students taught.

For each question submitted, you'll receive:

  • A mark for each answer (and an overall grade if submitting a full paper)
  • Detailed KAEA feedback — Knowledge, Application, Analysis, Evaluation
  • Comments on essay structure and technique
  • Clear, specific guidance on what to improve and how

Feedback is returned as a PDF by email.

After payment you'll be directed to a confirmation page with submission instructions. Email your work to eliotkingtuition@gmail.com. Accepted formats:

  • PDF or image scans
  • Word documents
  • Clear photos of handwritten work

Standard turnaround is 48 hours from when payment is received or work is received — whichever is later. For most students this means feedback arrives within a day or two of submitting.
