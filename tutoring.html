Common Questions

What happens in a typical session?

Sessions are personalised to each student’s needs, focusing on building understanding, improving essay technique, and practising exam-style questions with detailed feedback.

Can I change or cancel sessions?

Yes! You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your session. Lessons cancelled less than 24h in advance will be subject to a 100% lesson cancellation fee.

Where do lessons take place?

Lessons take place on a platform called thelessonspace.com. Once you have booked a session, you will receive an email and calendar invite with the details.

Other tutoring options?

If you have are seeking support that does not match the options above, we can still help! Please book a free meeting or use the Contact page to describe the support you are looking for and we will be in touch shortly.
